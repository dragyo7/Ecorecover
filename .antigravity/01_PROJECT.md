# Elevator Pitch

EcoRecover is an AI-powered Android application that makes e-waste recycling as fast and convenient as ordering groceries.

Inspired by Blinkit, Instamart, and Uber, EcoRecover enables users to instantly search electronic devices, receive transparent live valuations based on recoverable metals, schedule doorstep pickups, track Eco Partners in real time, and receive rewards for sustainable recycling.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Problem Statement

Most people have unused electronic devices but rarely recycle them because:

• Recycling centres are difficult to find.

• Pricing lacks transparency.

• Pickup is inconvenient.

• Users do not trust valuation.

• Recycling feels slow and outdated.

EcoRecover removes these barriers through a fast, transparent and modern experience.
