------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Elevator Pitch

EcoRecover is an on-demand e-waste pickup platform that combines the speed and convenience of Blinkit, the live service experience of Uber, and a transparent valuation engine powered by real-time recoverable metal prices.

Users can instantly search devices, receive live quotes, schedule pickups, track the assigned Eco Partner in real time, and receive payment after inspection.
